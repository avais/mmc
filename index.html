Mubarukh Medical Complex New Block

In the city like sargodha, where we lack the tons of facilities to cure the various stages of diseases like preventive, causative, promotive & curative. Three years back we launched the mubarak hospital to help our citizens. Fast forward to today, after gaining the faith & confidence of people in sargodha & uncurbed efforts of our highly qualified health professionals, we are pleased to announce that we have decided to launch the newblock mubarak hospital, all laced with the latest technology & all sort of medical specialities so citizens of sargodha can avail all health care facilities under a single roof. Looking forward to better & healthy days.
